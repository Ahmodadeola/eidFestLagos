WHAT IS EIDFEST LAGOS?

EidFest Lagos is a one-day event that targets 1,500 muslims out of over 13 million muslims in Lagos, in the spirit of love and unity after 29/30 days of fulfilling an obligatory act of worship. The event converges muslims in a space of funfair, network, picnic, sales aimed at celebrating the end of the holy month of ramadan, Eid-ul-fitr. The event promises to be filled with fun with different features where you get to commemorate and meet with lots of people, get a lot to eat and drink halal, learn something new and shop varieties of halal items. The event insha Allah, will hold on the day of eid-ul-fitr at Muri Okunola park, Victoria Island, Lagos and we look forward to seeing you and your loved ones at the venue that day insha Allah. Hide
